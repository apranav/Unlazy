Privacy Policy for Unlazy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Unlazy application for mobile devices. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and device permissions.

1. Data Collection and Local Storage

Unlazy is a privacy-first, local-only application.

We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data. All data you enter into the app—including tasks, routines, categories, and productivity statistics—is stored locally and securely on your device using a local database. Your data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us or any third-party servers.

If the app utilizes any on-device Artificial Intelligence (AI) to assist with task breakdown or productivity, all processing occurs entirely locally on your device's hardware. No prompts or task data are sent to external cloud AI providers.

2. Device Permissions

To provide core functionality, Unlazy requests specific device permissions. These permissions are used strictly to operate the app's features and never to collect data for external use:

  • Notifications: Required to send you reminders for your scheduled tasks and routines.
  • Exact Alarms: Required to ensure your time-sensitive routine reminders and Focus Mode timers fire accurately, even when the device is idle or in Doze mode.
  • Calendar (If applicable): Required only if you choose to sync your tasks with your device's native calendar. This data remains on your device.

3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

Unlazy does not integrate with third-party tracking software, advertising networks, or remote analytics services. We do not track your usage behavior across other apps or websites.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because all data is stored locally, you maintain complete control over it. You can delete individual tasks or routines within the app at any time. If you wish to delete all data associated with Unlazy, you can simply uninstall the application or clear the app's storage data in your device's system settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since no data is transmitted to us, parents do not need to contact us to delete data; simply uninstalling the app will remove all associated local data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
